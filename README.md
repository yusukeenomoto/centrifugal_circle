# Centrifugal Circle Shattering Art

This Processing sketch visualizes a solid circle being torn apart by centrifugal forces and organic noise.

## Concept
1. **Initial State**: A dense collection of particles forming a circle.
2. **Centrifugal Dispersion**: When activated, particles are pushed outward from the center.
3. **Perlin Shredding**: Instead of a perfect explosion, Perlin noise modulates the forces, causing the circle to "shred" into complex, thread-like or shard-like structures.
4. **Interaction**: Click the screen to trigger the "centrifugation" process or reset.
